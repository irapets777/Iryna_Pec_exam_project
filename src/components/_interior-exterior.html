Design VISION AVTR - the epitome of sustainability

The pioneering VISION AVTR concept combines the sustainable interaction of interior design, exterior design and UX. The entire design process was focused on a specific result - a unique experience of interaction and perception of the concept car by the driver and passengers.

The outer side opening extends through the interior and creates an endless loop inspired by the sacred connection between the Na'vi people in the movie Avatar and their natural habitat. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with the vehicle and the outside world. Thus, having gone from the concept of awareness to the unique fusion of man and car.

Also, with its elongated "One Bow" design and organic design philosophy, the VISION AVTR represents a radical new stage in the automotive industry. Thus, having gone from the concept of awareness to the unique fusion of man and car, Mercedes-Benz engineers succeeded in creating the VISION AVTR concept electric vehicle.

A concise continuation of the external minimalist design are the front seats, made in a very organic form, which resembles leafy hammocks from the planet Pandora. The center console symbolizes the Tree of Souls, the Na'vi's most sacred site. The control unit is intuitive and incredibly functional. It was about creating a unique space in which passengers have a biometric connection with each other, with the vehicle and the outside world.

In addition, the control center is located in such a way that it is easily accessible to both the driver and passenger, as well as rear passengers.

In this way, VISION AVTR establishes a close biometric connection with the driver and helps to increase awareness of the surrounding situation.

The back of the seats and the interior of the roof are made of a special fabric that changes color. Depending on the lighting, textiles can be dark blue or delicate light blue.

But beyond that, the eco-friendliness of the interior of the electric car is further enhanced with DINAMICA® vegan leather, the first and only microfiber that guarantees eco-efficiency throughout the entire production cycle.

DINAMICA® is recycled material from old clothes, fabrics and PET plastic bottles.
